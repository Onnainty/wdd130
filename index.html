Rio Negro

Río Negro is a province of Argentina, in Patagonia. It borders Neuquen to the north and Chubut to the south. It also borders Chile to the west. Río Negro has the most popular tourist city in the country: Bariloche, with lakes and mountains, where Cerro Catedral is also located, which is one of the most famous sky centers in all of South America.
