History

White Water Rafting was founded in 1982 by outdoor enthusiast and river guide, Sarah Thompson. Inspired by her love for nature and adrenaline-pumping adventures, Sarah envisioned a company that could share the exhilaration of white water rafting with others while promoting environmental stewardship. Starting with just a handful of rafts and a small team of dedicated guides, the company began offering trips on the challenging rapids of rivers around the world.

Today, White Water Rafting is a leader in the adventure tourism industry, renowned for its thrilling expeditions and unwavering dedication to environmental responsibility. With operations spanning multiple countries and a diverse range of offerings, the company continues to inspire adventurers of all ages to explore and protect the world's waterways. Through its ongoing efforts, White Water Rafting remains true to Sarah Thompson's original vision: to share the beauty and excitement of white water rafting while promoting a deep respect for nature.
